Solving the "Last Mile" problem for crypto wealth.

Despite the growth of digital assets, "off-ramping"—converting crypto to fiat for daily spend—remains a high-friction, high-fee pain point. Users are often forced onto prepaid debit cards with limited acceptance or clunky top-up flows.

imToken Card aims to disrupt this by offering a true credit card experience that allows users to spend first and pay bills later with their crypto assets, bridging the gap between Web3 wealth and the Web2 payment infrastructure (Mastercard/Visa network).

"Trustworthy & Economic"

In a market saturated with high-fee, unreliable "crypto cards," the design strategy focused on building immediate trust and highlighting economic value.

  • Trustworthy: Visuals and copy that align with established banking standards (Partnered with DCS/Mastercard) to reduce user anxiety about fund safety.
  • "Easy" as a Benchmark: The UX must match or beat the simplicity of a standard bank card. No complex "DeFi" jargon—just "Spend" and "Pay Bill."
  • Resonating & Motivating: Using the UI to highlight the unique value of paying with crypto (e.g., "You saved X fees today") and gamifying the tiering system to encourage engagement.
