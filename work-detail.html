imToken 3.0

Redesigning the next generation crypto wallet experience for millions of users.

Context

imToken is one of the world's most popular non-custodial wallets. As the ecosystem evolved, users needed more than just a place to store keys—they needed a gateway to DeFi and Web3.

Problem

The existing architecture was fragmented effectively creating silos between different chains (ETH, BTC, EOS). Users found it difficult to manage a holistic portfolio or engage with cross-chain applications.

Problem Illustration (Image/GIF)

Solution

We introduced a new "Identity-first" architecture, allowing users to manage assets across chains under a single identity. We also completely overhauled the design system to be more approachable and secure.
