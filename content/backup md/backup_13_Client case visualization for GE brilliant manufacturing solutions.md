# Client case visualization for GE brilliant manufacturing solutions

Demonstrate digitalization in traditional manufacturing through interactive data visualization.

## About the project

I created client facing data visualization to demonstrate the strength and mechanism of GE Digital solution applications in traditional manufacturing. Clients' cases are showed in a curved screen with interactions so they can be guided through details of each section.

![Project overview](assets/images/projects/ge/637ba4639093ce8ecfb2f808_feature.png)

## Approaches and results

In this project, I collected and transformed all key business data. Based on understanding of technological and business terms, I visualized them and animated them with front end engineer, to make it straightforward and persuasive.

The visualization helped clients understand the complex manufacturing data and GE's digital solutions in an intuitive and engaging way.
